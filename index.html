What About Siraisan ?

Siraisan is the name of a village in Ulu Barumun District, Padanglawas (Palas) Regency, North Sumatra.

The Barumun (Aek) River which divides Siraisan Village is just under the old Ulu Aer bridge, connecting Sibuhuan, the capital city of Palas, with Aekgodang (Aekgodang Airport), North Padanglawas Regency (Paluta)

What About Danau Tao ?
